Maples Rugs Pelham Vintage Runner Rug

Bring a touch of vintage elegance to your living space with this beautifully crafted runner rug. Inspired by traditional scroll patterns, its intricate design adds a warm, sophisticated aesthetic to any room. Available in rich wineberry hues of charcoal and radiant blue, it seamlessly integrates with classic, transitional, and modern decor to create a timeless look.

Engineered for everyday use, this rug is built to last. Its 100% polyester pile ensures durability and resilience, making it perfect for high-traffic areas like hallways and entryways. Handcrafted in the USA by Maples Rugs, this rug is a testament to quality and heritage. Every detail is carefully considered to deliver both comfort and style, ensuring it's a reliable and cherished addition to your home.
